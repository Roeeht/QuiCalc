# QuiCalc
PlanY is a tool for architects and private clients that provides solutions to various real estate problems.
The product provides architecture services.
The founding team includes an experienced architect and two entrepreneurs with a vision to build a potential property using an algorithm based on existing architectural plans at the click of a button.

Currently, there is no platform for individuals and professionals who want to budget the renovation of their home or client's home before the renovation.
In addition, there is a problem in coordinating expectations between the client and the architect on the financial side, which causes problems in the execution of the architectural plan.
We, as part of the PlanY project, provide a platform which solves the above problems.
Our platform provides budget estimates for architectural plans and allows the user to perform a number of actions:

• Selection of items and actions that he wants to perform or purchase during the renovation by a user-friendly form.
• At the click of a button the user can get a detailed view which will show the costs for the items which he selected.
• With the click of a button the user can get the total cost of the renovation.
• Depending on the user's choices, the platform will offer the user renovation options appropriate to his choices.

The technologies we used:
